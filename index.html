Najib is known for his energetic performances, often displaying elements of his multicultural background into his comedy.


Najib went on another venture starting a podcast called "Wat kan er nou gebeuren" on Youtube. The podcast provides a platform for Najib to explore a wide range of topics, from personal stories and life lessons to societal issues and cultural insights. What sets "Wat kan er nou gebeuren" apart is its focus on inviting guests with unusual backgrounds and experiences, offering listeners a unique and often thought-provoking perspective on the world.
